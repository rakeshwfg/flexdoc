Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate my Pro license?
After purchasing, you'll receive a license key via email. Activate it using one of three methods:
  • Set environment variable: FLEXDOC_LICENSE_KEY=your-key
  • In code: new FlexDoc({ licenseKey: 'your-key' })
  • Add to .env file: FLEXDOC_LICENSE_KEY=your-key
Can I use the free version commercially?
Yes! The free version is fully open-source and can be used for commercial projects. However, advanced features like Professional Mode, premium themes, and cloud storage require a Pro license.
What's the difference between standard and professional mode?
Professional mode uses ML-powered layout optimization for Adobe-quality presentations. It's 4x faster (no browser overhead), produces better layouts, and includes enhanced visual hierarchy. This is a Pro-only feature.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with FlexDoc Pro, contact us within 30 days for a full refund.
How does volume licensing work for Enterprise?
Enterprise plans include volume licensing for teams. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs, number of seats, and custom pricing.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can upgrade from Free to Pro or Enterprise at any time. For downgrades, contact support and we'll help you transition smoothly.
Is there a lifetime license option?
Yes! Contact us for lifetime Pro license pricing. Lifetime licenses include all Pro features and updates forever, with no recurring fees.
